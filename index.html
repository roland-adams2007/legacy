The Competition

The Legacy234 Paint & Sip Competition is a dynamic celebration of Nigeria's history through art. 100 teams, each with a canvas, come together to create masterpieces that document our great country's legacy.

Enjoy wine or juice while your team follows guided painting sessions in a lively, inspiring environment. It's about collaboration, creativity, and celebrating Nigeria's rich history with every brushstroke.

Join us to party, paint, and be part of a historic moment where art meets legacy.

Documenting Nigeria's history, one canvas at a time. Join the Legacy234 Paint & Sip Competition.
